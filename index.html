What is BudgetPro

The system which aims to simplify executives and CFOs to model their budgets. It allows you to quickly adapt the budget to drastic changes in the economic environment, find a break-even point and adjust costs to it. The system also has the ability to quickly calculate the scale of the business.

How we started

1. The idea

Unusual situation caused by the COVID-19 has suspended business development and jeopardized their financial well-being. The co-founder of the company Natalia Kyrnychna, well-experienced in building the financial system of the company, helped her clients to build and manage the 2020 budgets. She thought about the system which could replace the long manual analysis and help to model a budget with just a few clicks. She came up with idea to create a system that would help automatically model the budget with the priority usage and a basic unit of measurement creation.

2. Support

Later, Volodymyr joined Natalia. He is a software development engineer with a long-term experience. He’s been fascinated by her idea and quickly took the first steps in developing the system. In this tandem, the startup BudgetPro was born.

3. Action!

The system has the ability not only to model the budget in the direction of reducing and optimizing costs, but also offers steps to scale the business, find a break-even point or simplify the work of CEO, CFO and financial and analytical department of the enterprise.
